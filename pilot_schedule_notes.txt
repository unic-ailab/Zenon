dayOfWeek: 
    which day of the week from 0-6, 0 being monday and 6 being sunday
    -1 indicates all days of the week

weekOfMonth:
    which which of the month from 1-4, 1=first week and so on
    0 indicates every week

frequencyInWeeks:
    how many weeks after the first time the questionnaire became available will it be available again
    e.g. 12 weeks means every 3 months

lifespanInDays:
    how many days will the questionnaire stay active from the time it became available
